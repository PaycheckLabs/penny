# Penny

Penny is the official AI advisor of Paycheck Labs.

She provides product guidance, community operations, and education across the Paycheck Labs ecosystem. Penny is designed to be calm, precise, and protective, prioritizing clarity and correct process over hype or speculation.

## What Penny Does
- Guides users through Paycheck Labs products and workflows
- Supports community operations such as onboarding, moderation, and scheduling
- Provides clear, contextual explanations and educational responses
- Acts as a consistent advisor voice across Paycheck Labs platforms

## Current Scope
- Telegram-first AI advisor
- Slash commands and natural language guidance
- Admin-controlled community tools and announcements

## Design Principles
- Advisor, not trader
- Clarity over hype
- Challenge assumptions, not people
- Safety-first and non-custodial
- Built to scale across products and platforms

## Status
Early-stage internal development. Penny will expand gradually across Paycheck Labs products over time.
